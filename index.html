Platform Intelligence Q&A

How does AuraSync differ from traditional project tools?

Unlike static tools like Trello or Asana, AuraSync uses a predictive engine. It analyzes your team's past performance to provide accurate completion dates and automatically identifies team burnout before it happens.

Is my data used to train the AI?

Absolutely not. Your data is your own. Our Enterprise agreement guarantees that your data stays within your private cloud instance and is never used to train our global models.

Do you offer integration with Slack or Microsoft Teams?

Yes, we have deep integrations with over 200+ popular enterprise apps, allowing you to update tasks and receive AI summaries directly within your existing chat workflows.
